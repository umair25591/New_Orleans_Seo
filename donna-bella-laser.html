Donna Bella Laser Case Study: Ranking 1st on Google Search and Google Maps

Client

donnabellalaser.com

Business Type

Medical Spa

Services Provided

SEO and Content Marketing

Engagement Period

Started Early 2024



Before Working With Us

Before working with us, Donna Bella Laser wasn't visible on Google. Their market has incredible search volume but they were letting their competitors rake in all the traffic by not investing in SEO. This SEO campaign began with a weak foundation:

  • Unintentional site architecture
  • Lack of on-page or technical SEO
  • No blog page
  • 3/100 domain authority
  • ~100 monthly search traffic
  • No leads from Google




Our SEO Strategy For Success

We had to start our SEO campaign by building a strong foundation for the website. We started by fixing indexing issues, doing technical SEO, rebuilding site architecture, and UI/UX work BEFORE we began to touch their content, links, and creating new pages. After a we built the foundation, we started the nitty gritty of our advanced SEO and began:

  • Analyzed their competitor's link profiles
  • Sourced quality backlinks
  • Created blog content
  • Inter-linked well to distribute link juice and traffic



Campaign Results





Within 10 months we took the website from INVISIBLE on Google search to:

  • Ranking #1 for "laser hair removal" on Google Search AND Google Maps (See screenshot)
  • Over 3,000 monthly organic search traffic (all from SEO)
  • 35+ qualified leads per month
  • Increased brand awareness and reputation locally
