Before Working With Us

Before working with us, Lemon Law Assist was invisible on Google. This market has incredible search volume and yet they were letting their competitors rake in all the traffic. We started this SEO campaign with a very weak foundation:

  • Poor site architecture
  • No on-page or technical SEO
  • No blog page
  • 2/100 domain authority
  • ~150 monthly search traffic
  • Zero Google leads




Our SEO Strategy For Success

We had to start our SEO campaign by building a strong foundation for the website. This meant fixing indexing issues, technical SEO, site architecture, and even basic UI/UX work BEFORE even starting to touch content, backlinks, or new pages. After a strong foundation was developed, we got into the nitty gritty of SEO and began:

  • Audited competitor backlink profiles
  • Acquired quality backlinks
  • Published blog content
  • Inter-linked well to spread link juice and traffic



Campaign Results





Within 10 months we took the website from INVISIBLE on Google search to:

  • Over 4,000 monthly organic search traffic (all from SEO)
  • 25.7 clicks and 822k total impressions
  • 93+ top 3 Google keyword rankings
  • 30+ legal leads each month
  • The most well-known law firm in their local market
